01About the firm

A client-centric legal concierge.

Established in 1997, Nasheed & Co is a leading Maldivian law firm — a team of young, vibrant lawyers led by an experienced hand, delivering efficient, convenient and responsive service across the full spectrum of corporate and commercial law.

Who we are

Lawyers specialising in corporate and commercial law. We act as a legal concierge — empathising with the client, understanding the pain points, and working alongside you to reach tailored solutions.

What we do

We provide corporate and commercial law advice and support, guided by our core values. We are driven to deliver — honest and transparent, patient and helpful, respectful and fair.

Where we are

In Malé, right behind the south wall of the Maldives Parliament House. Can’t make it in? In-person and remote consultations are both available — just call or email.
