01About us

A client-centric legal concierge, since 1997.

Nasheed & Co is a leading Maldivian law firm — a team of young, vibrant lawyers led by an experienced hand, delivering efficient, convenient and responsive service across the full spectrum of corporate and commercial law.

Lawyers specialising in corporate and commercial law. We act as a legal concierge — empathising with the client, understanding the pain points, and working alongside you to reach tailored solutions.

What we do

We provide corporate and commercial law advice and support, guided by our core values. We are driven to deliver — honest and transparent, patient and helpful, respectful and fair.

02Practice areas

How we can help.

Two divisions, one accountable team. Select a division to see the areas we cover — engagements are scoped to your needs, from a single transaction to ongoing retained counsel.

Corporate Law

Corporate law is one of our focus areas, with a wide arena of transactions, experience and expertise. We aid with the incorporation of companies every step of the way, and provide compliance advice and updated procedural steps to our corporate clients.

Foreign Investment (Inbound)

Our experience and connections enable us to provide a concierge service to those interested in investing in the Maldives. We bring forth practical solutions for the business objectives of our clients, within legal parameters.

Hotels, Resorts & Tourism

Our lawyers provide well-rounded services to hospitality clients both new and existing in the industry — construction period extension, compliance with the tourism law, sale and purchase, and more.

General Commercial

Contracts and agreements are at the heart of commercial transactions. We draft, negotiate and execute agreements which protect the client’s every interest.

Banking & Financing

We provide solutions to a myriad of challenges faced by our clients, navigating them through the process of borrowing with expert knowledge of the law in this sector.

Mergers & Acquisitions

Our team takes a solution-oriented approach with local expertise to maximise deal value. We have engaged in several M&A matters, helping us understand and overcome the obstacles in every phase of the transaction.

Employment & Labour

Managing a workforce means staying up to date with local laws. We layer this knowledge onto our own and provide swift responses to queries and concerns such as work-visa applications.

Insurance

We provide pragmatic, strategic advice on complex insurance transactions — including the probability of success of a claim based on the insurance regulatory framework.

Tax (GST / TGST)

We advise on the possible tax implications of commercial transactions, and provide innovative solutions to our clients’ current involvements in this area.

Litigation

The firm has handled a vast variety of complex cases, securing a win for the majority. Our familiarity with the battlefield allows us to guide clients to their desired outcome, with the best dispute-resolution strategy when an obstacle arises.

Arbitration

We offer a seamless solution to complex disputes outside of the court. Our team has experience liaising with foreign arbitrators and advising them on Maldives law as required by the rules of arbitration.

Mediation

We provide mediation services to clients who wish to avoid aggressive litigation and settle amicably — resolving conflicts while maintaining neutrality, to reach the most beneficial result for both parties.

Investigations

Where a client is uncertain of the legal terrain, we specialise in obtaining, reviewing and analysing data from multiple sources — providing the most accurate and clear picture on which to take well-informed decisions.

05FAQs

Frequently asked questions.

Answers to the questions we’re asked most. Don’t see yours? Just get in touch.

What areas of law does Nasheed & Co practice?

We are a corporate and commercial law firm. Our work spans two divisions — Corporate (corporate law, foreign investment, hotels & tourism, banking & finance, M&A, employment, insurance and tax) and Litigation (litigation, arbitration, mediation and investigations).

How do I start working with the firm?

Reach out via the contact form, phone or email. We’ll arrange a brief, confidential meeting to understand your matter, then send a tailored engagement letter with a defined scope and transparent pricing before any work begins.

Do you advise foreign investors?

Yes. We provide a concierge service to those investing in the Maldives — bringing practical solutions to your business objectives within the legal framework, from structuring to approvals and compliance.

Can consultations be done remotely?

Absolutely. In-person and remote consultations are both available. If you can’t make it into the office, simply call or email us and we’ll arrange a convenient time.

How are your fees structured?

Engagements are scoped to your needs — a single transaction handled, or an ongoing retained relationship. Every engagement letter sets out a defined scope and transparent pricing, so there are no surprises.

Where is your office?
